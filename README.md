# Stock market Web app

## This web app is basically a showcase of different graphs of stock market indecies and stocks of other companies.
#
## **The attributes/legends in the graph are :**
## *1. Open*
## *2. Close*
## *3. Volume*
## *4. Date(years)*
## *5. Adj-close*
## *6. High*
